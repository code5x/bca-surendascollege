About Suren Das College (Autonomous)

Suren Das College is a higher education institute located in Hajo, Assam. It was established to provide quality education and has become a center for academic and cultural growth. The college focuses on building students’ knowledge, practical skills, and values to prepare them for future challenges.

It offers undergraduate programs in Arts, Science, and Commerce, along with courses in Computer Science, vocational studies, and other skill-based programs for modern careers. Students are also encouraged to join extracurricular activities like sports, cultural events, and community services for overall development.

The college has modern classrooms, a library, laboratories, and other facilities that support effective learning. It is affiliated with Gauhati University and continues to grow as an important center of higher education in Assam.

For more information, visit Suren Das College Official Website.

Computer Science Department

The Computer Science Department at Suren Das College gives students a solid foundation in computer science and related fields. It offers undergraduate courses like BCA, along with programs in computer applications, programming, and information technology. Students gain skills in coding, problem-solving, and software applications, preparing them for careers in the IT field.

Practical learning is supported by the computer lab, where students practice coding, work on small projects, and explore applications of their learning. Though modest, the lab provides effective hands-on experience, helping students build confidence and technical abilities.

About this Website

This website is a study hub designed to give students easy access to study materials. It collects ebooks, class notes, old question papers, syllabus, assignments, and other files in one place, so students spend less time searching and more time learning. Materials are organized by subject and semester for easy navigation.

The site mainly helps Computer Science students, especially BCA students, but also has general resources useful for other departments. Currently, most materials are available upto 2nd semester, and content for higher semesters will be added gradually. Students and professional can contribute by upload study notes and resources to keep the collection updated.

The website works well on mobile and tablets, making study convenient anywhere. It also shares links to trusted external resources such as digital libraries, learning portals, and YouTube playlists to support extra learning and revision for every students. Students can also search books from the college library, similar to an OPAC system, so they can quickly check book availability with ease.

Our Vision

Our goal is to build a simple, reliable study platform for college students. We aim to make learning materials easy to access, encourage sharing of notes, and provide tools that solve common study problems. Over time, the site will grow with more semesters, resources, and features for both classroom learning and self-study.
