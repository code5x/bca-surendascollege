Welcome to Suren Das College - Computer Science Department

The Department of Computer Science is a place for learning, exploring, and growing with technology. Through this website, students can access class notes, study materials, and other resources anytime. More than just a collection of resources, the site is built to guide students step by step, making it easier for them to understand topics and improve their skills beyond the classroom. Students can also contribute by sharing or uploading notes and resources, making learning simple, practical, and more effective for everyone.

While the main focus is on Computer Science, many of the study resources and materials available here can also support students from other departments. By offering useful notes and guidance, the site encourages learning that can benefit anyone interested in improving their knowledge.
